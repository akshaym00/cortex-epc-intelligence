VENDOR DELAY NOTICE — Data Hall A Fit-Out
Reference: VDN-2026-007
Date: 9 July 2026
Issued By: Vendor ABC
To: Project Team

Subject: Diesel Generator Delivery Delay — 7 Calendar Days

Vendor ABC has informed the Project Team that the Diesel Generator delivery to site
will be delayed by 7 calendar days due to a factory testing overrun and transport
scheduling conflict.

ENTITIES INVOLVED

Vendor ABC supplies the Diesel Generator to the Data Hall A project. Vendor ABC is
the primary equipment vendor for this package and maintains a direct supply
relationship with the Project Team.

The Diesel Generator is a critical piece of mechanical infrastructure required for
temporary power supply during Electrical Installation. The Diesel Generator must
be on site and commissioned before Electrical Installation can commence.

Generator Delivery is the scheduled site delivery activity managed by Vendor ABC.
Generator Delivery depends on Vendor ABC completing factory acceptance testing.
Generator Delivery is now delayed by 7 days.

Electrical Installation is a project activity owned by the Project Team. Electrical
Installation depends on Generator Delivery. Electrical Installation cannot begin
until the Diesel Generator has been delivered and accepted on site.

Commissioning Activities are the final project phase owned by the Project Team.
Commissioning Activities depend on Electrical Installation completion. Any delay to
Electrical Installation directly delays Commissioning Activities.

Procurement Approval is a milestone that must be obtained before Electrical
Installation can formally commence. Vendor ABC must resubmit a revised delivery
schedule before Procurement Approval can be granted.

IMPACT ASSESSMENT

Generator Delivery is delayed by 7 days. This directly delays Electrical
Installation by 7 days. Commissioning Activities are consequently delayed by 7
days. Procurement Approval may be deferred pending revised programme.

The Project Team must revise the programme and notify all downstream activity
owners of the 7-day slippage.

ACTIONS REQUIRED

Vendor ABC must provide a written recovery plan within 24 hours detailing revised
Generator Delivery dates and any mitigation measures.

The Project Team must review impact on Electrical Installation and Commissioning
Activities and issue updated programme dates to all stakeholders.

Procurement Approval should be withheld until Vendor ABC confirms the revised
Generator Delivery schedule.
